## Dataset
The raw dataset used in this project is the **LinkedIn Job Postings 
2023–2024** dataset, publicly available on Kaggle:

🔗 https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/arshkon/linkedin-job-postings

**Dataset Summary:**
- Raw records: 123,849
- After cleaning and deduplication: 123,478 records
- Final modelling dataset: 105,604 records
- Number of role categories: 38
- Skill dictionary size: 344 skills
